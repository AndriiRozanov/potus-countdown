Privacy Policy

Who we are

PresidencyClock is an independent, non-affiliated informational website available at presidencyclock.com.

What data we collect

  • Consent status from the cookie banner (via Google Funding Choices).
  • Ads & measurement data, only if you consent to cookies (Google AdSense/Ad Manager).
  • Server logs (IP address, user-agent, referrer) kept briefly for security and reliability.
  • Contact form submissions you send to us (optional).

Why we collect it

  • To show the site and keep it secure and reliable.
  • To fund development with privacy-respecting advertising.
  • To respond when you contact us.

Cookies & consent

We use Google’s Funding Choices to collect your consent and Google’s Consent Mode v2 to respect it. Until you consent, ad storage, ad user data, ad personalization and analytics storage are set to denied. You can change your choice anytime via your browser cookies (remove the consent cookie) and reload the page.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-5023415479696725). Depending on your consent and region, Google may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads. Learn more in Google’s Advertising Policy.

Data retention

Server logs are kept for a short, limited period. Contact form messages are kept as long as needed to handle your request.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
